Student trustees are elected officials that actively advocate for student voice. Student trustees are elected by the students within the school board to ensure proper communication of student issues and opinions. They are active participants in board meetings and may offer suggestions or motions (dependent on school board). The student reps campaign goal is to bring official student voice into the education system, at the district level. We are aiming to have a student representative in each school district within 2 years.
